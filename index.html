📜 Prashna Golden Rules

1. Be Specific & Clear
Avoid vague questions. Instead of "Will I get a job?", ask "Will I get a stable job within 3 months?"
2. Include a Timeframe
Always add a realistic timeframe (e.g., "within 6 months", "by year end").
3. Ask 1 Question at a Time
Do not bombard the cosmos with multiple queries. Focus on one urgent need.
4. Sincerity is Key
The chart reflects your mental energy. Distraction or insincerity leads to unclear answers.
5. Option A vs Option B
The 1st House represents Option A; the 7th House represents Option B. The stronger lord wins.
